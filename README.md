# ALTITUD HUB - Operations & Intelligence Platform

## Overview
ALTITUD HUB is a premium operations dashboard and business intelligence tool for RE/MAX Altitud and Altitud Cero. It provides agents with a unified interface for managing pre-listings, tracking their business OKRs, performing Comparative Market Analysis (ACM), and accessing AI-driven business mentorship.

## Features
- **Dashboard OKR**: The central hub for agents to track their pipeline, capture targets, and monitor overall business health and daily habits.
- **Operations Center**: Real-time management of listings, deals, and transaction lifecycles under the `Negocio` module.
- **Tools & Utilities**: Includes CRM capabilities (Contact management), intelligent Pre-Listing data capture, and customized ACM property valuation generation.
- **ALTITUD AI Mentor (Olympia)**: Intelligent business feedback, automated contract parsing, and motivated coaching for real estate agents.

## Design Philosophy
The platform follows the "Excellent" design language:
- **Slate-50 Palette**: High-contrast, clean, and professional light mode.
- **Bold Typography**: Signature italic headers for a premium, high-impact feel.
- **Glassmorphism**: Subtle translucency, refined shadows, and dynamic micro-interactions.

## Tech Stack
- Next.js (App Router)
- Tailwind CSS
- Supabase (Backend & Auth)
- custom JavaScript (Legacy Modules Integration)
